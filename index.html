SAGRNN: Self-Attentive Based Gated RNNs for Binaural Speaker Separation with Interaural Cue Preservation

Ke Tan1, Buye Xu2, Anurag Kumar2, Eliya Nachmani3, 4, Yossi Adi3

1The Ohio State University, 2Facebook Reality Labs, 3Facebook AI Research, 4Tel-Aviv University

Intro

In this post, we present a deep model named SAGRNN for binaural speaker separation with interaural cue preservation.

In this study, the goal of binaural speaker separation is to estimate C concurrent binaural speech signals s1, ..., sC from a sound mixture y. We assume that there is no room reverberation in the acoustic environment.
